Aditya Devarakonda

Assistant Professor
Department of Computer Science
Wake Forest University

251 Manchester Hall
devaraa@wfu.edu

My research interests lie in sparse linear algebra, high performance computing, parallel algorithms, and numerical methods. I am particularly interested in developing efficient algorithms and software for solving sparse nonlinear problems, which are fundamental to many scientific and engineering applications. I also work on optimizing the performance of these algorithms on parallel architectures, including GPUs, clouds, and supercomputers.

I am actively looking for collaborators and motivated students.
